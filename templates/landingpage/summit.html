About the Summit

The Kenya Software Summit is a landmark annual event bringing together developers, startups, global technology leaders, policymakers, and investors. The summit aims to position Kenya as Africa’s premier hub for software innovation and AI leadership.

Summit Features

Keynote Addresses

Insights from global leaders, executives, and policymakers.

Hackathons & Challenges

Solving real-world problems across fintech, health, and agritech.

Tech Expo

Showcasing cutting-edge innovations, platforms, and APIs.

Agenda Overview

  • Day 1: Policy & Innovation, Keynotes
  • Day 2: Workshops, Hackathons, Talent Pavilion
  • Day 3: Startup Demos, Tech Expo, Closing

Partners & Organizers
