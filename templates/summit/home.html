The Flagship Software & AI Summit

Kenya's and Africa's digital transformation agenda presents a unique opportunity to leapfrog development stages, increased opportunities for innovation and entrepreneurship, fostering inclusive growth as well as strengthen competitiveness in the global digital economy.

This is anchored on talented youthful population, thriving ICT sector and fast-growing digital economy supported by Government Policies, Private Sector initiatives, Academia and development partners support, strategically positioning Kenya to lead Africa‚Äôs next wave of digital transformation.

To realize this agenda, the Ministry of Information, Communications and the Digital Economy in partners with Africa Telecommunications Union is hosting The Kenya Software & AI Summit, an annual event attracting stakeholders from various sectors all over the world.

November 10-12 2025


The summit provides a platform for dialogue, knowledge exchange and partnership building in the Software, Artificial Intelligence and other emerging technologies Sectors.

This Year 2025, the Summit will be held at Moi University, Annex Campus Eldoret City.

Chief Guest

His Excellency Dr. William Samoei Ruto, C.G.H.,

President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces.
